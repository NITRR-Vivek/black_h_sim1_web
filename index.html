Mission Briefing: Black Hole Explorer

Welcome, Astronaut! You are about to visit a Black Hole.

A Black Hole is an object in space with gravity so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape!

How to Explore:

  • W A S D: Move your astronaut.
  • Space / Shift: Fly Up / Down.
  • Mouse: Look around.
  • 1, 2, 3: Teleport (Orbit, Disk, Jets).

What to look for:

  • Event Horizon: The black sphere. Once you cross it, you can never come back!
  • Accretion Disk: The glowing ring of hot gas spinning around the hole.
  • Spaghettification: If you get too close, gravity stretches you like a noodle!
